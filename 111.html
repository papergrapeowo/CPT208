Echoes of Suzhou

Digital Narrative Guide System for Suzhou Museum

Module 01

Motivation & Research

1.1 Project Motivation

"Current museum visiting experiences face prominent limitations. Visitors can hardly observe cultural relics in detail, while traditional interpretations are rigid and lack interactivity..."

To enhance the accessibility of digital heritage, this project develops a lightweight web application featuring 3D artifact modeling and AI Q&A as core functions.

1.2 Academic Research Analysis

Paper 1: XR Exhibit+: AI-Enhanced XR Guide

Focus: XR (AR/VR) + LLM

▼
Strengths
  • Implements core 3D/XR visualization interaction paradigms.
  • Semantic-based knowledge association via knowledge graphs.
Limitations
  • Relies on dedicated XR hardware headsets.
  • Weak mobile AR fidelity impacts artifact details.
Implications: Draw on 3D optimization; focus on mini-program deployment.

Paper 2: Intelligent Guide Based on OFA

Focus: Cultural Relic Visual Q&A

▼
Strengths
  • Direct AI Q&A via exhibit photography scenarios.
  • Simulates real questioning logic during visits.
Limitations
  • Lacks 3D modeling and immersive display links.
  • Performance highly dependent on lighting quality.

Paper 3: Indoor Interactive Experience Design

Focus: 3D Reconstruction & Positioning

▼
Strengths
  • Complete technical path for 3D relic rendering.
  • Engineering implementation provides reference framework.
Limitations
  • Lacks support for open-ended AI interaction.
  • High learning cost for elderly or first-time users.

Paper 4: Natural Interaction with LLMs

Focus: Enhancing UX in Virtual Heritage

▼
Strengths
  • Supports smooth conversational interaction.
  • User-centered iterative design methodology.
Limitations
  • Developed for VR; no direct mobile support.
  • Latency issues in large-scale user scenarios.

1.3 Commercial Product Analysis

Dazhong Dianping

Life Service Platform

▼
Strengths
  • Large user base and strong brand influence.
  • One-stop ticketing and user review system.
Limitations
  • Auxiliary service; lacks immersion.
  • Limited interaction with cultural relics.

Audio Guide Device

Hardware Voice Guide

▼
Strengths
  • Professional and authoritative knowledge.
  • Stable hardware use during visit.
Limitations
  • Fixed content; lacks interactive Q&A.
  • Manual input of numbers is cumbersome.

Sanmao You App

Cultural Tourism App

▼
Strengths
  • Covers 15,000+ global sites.
  • AI relic recognition and VR tours.
Limitations
  • Complex charging; requires installation.
  • Mainly passive listening interaction.

SZ Museum Mini Program

Official Reservation

▼
Strengths
  • Accurate time-sharing reservations.
  • Lightweight mini program deployment.
Limitations
  • Single focus on reservation services.
  • Lacks 3D and immersive explanation.
